Recipe #1 - cake

  • Time: 50 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
all purpose flour 2 cups

baking soda 1 and 1/3 tsp
vanilla essence 1 and 1/3 tsp

Recipe #2 - pasta

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
penne pasta 1/2 lb
yellow onion 1
garlic cloves 2
olive oil 1 tbsp
diced tomatoes 115 oz
dried oregano 1/2 tsp
salt 1/2 tsp
tomato paste 2 tbsp
cream cheeze 2 oz
grated Parmesan 1/4 cup
fresh spinnach 4 oz

Step 1: Bring a large pot of water to a boil over high heat. Add the pasta and continue to boil until tender (7-10 minutes). Drain the pasta in a colander.
Step 2: While the pasta is cooking, prepare the creamy tomato sauce. Dice the onion and mince the garlic.
Step 3:Add the diced tomatoes (with juices), oregano, basil, crushed red pepper, salt, and some freshly cracked pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine. Add the tomato paste and a 1/2 cup of water to the skillet and stir until the tomato paste has mixed into the sauce.
Step 4:Turn the heat down to low. Cut the cream cheese into a few pieces and then add them to the skillet with the tomato sauce. Use a whisk to stir the sauce until the cream cheese has fully melted and the sauce is creamy.
Step 5:Add the fresh spinach to the skillet and gently stir it into the sauce until it has wilted (2-3 minutes). Add the pasta and stir until it is well coated in the creamy tomato sauce.

Recipe #3 - tomato basil soup

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 1 tbsp
diced onion 1
minced garlic cloves 3
chopped fresh tomatoes 7 cups
fresh basil 1 large handful
sea salt 2 teaspoons
ground black pepper 1 teaspoon

Step 1: Put the oil in a large pan and heat on medium.
Step 2: Once the oil is hot add the onions and cook for 2 to 3 minutes until translucent and just starting to turn golden on the edges. Then add the garlic and cook for 1 minute more, stirring frequently.
Step 3: Add the chopped tomatoes. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 to 6 minutes until the tomatoes are simmering and just starting to break down.
Step 4: Remove from the heat and add the basil, salt and pepper then blend with an immersion blender in the pan.
